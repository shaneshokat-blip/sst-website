Our Co-Curricular Programme

At SST, education goes beyond textbooks. Our students engage in a wide variety of activities that develop leadership, teamwork, creativity, and civic responsibility.

Debate & Public Speaking

Students participate in Urdu and English debate competitions, MUN sessions, and oratory events — building confidence and critical thinking.

Urdu DebateEnglish DebateMUN

Sports & Athletics

Inter-house sports competitions including cricket, football, volleyball, badminton, table tennis and annual athletics day.

CricketFootballAthleticsBadminton

Arts & Crafts

Creative expression through painting, calligraphy, model-making and art exhibitions. Students develop aesthetic sense and artistic talent.

PaintingCalligraphyModel Making

Science Competitions

Participants in national/international science olympiads, FSP contests, and provincial science fairs — consistently winning top positions.

Science OlympiadFSP ContestQuiz Bowl

Community Service

Students give back through outreach programs, health awareness drives, Ramadan welfare activities, and community engagement.

Welfare DrivesHealth CampsOutreach

Nasheeds & Performing Arts

National day performances, nasheed competitions, drama and theatrical presentations that develop confidence and stage presence.

NasheedsDramaTableaux

Model UN (MUN)

SST hosts its own MUN — Yarians MUN Contest — where students represent world nations and debate real global issues diplomatically.

DiplomacyGlobal IssuesLeadership

National Day Celebrations

Grand celebrations on Independence Day, Defence Day, Iqbal Day, and Pakistan Day with flags, speeches, and patriotic events.

14 AugustDefence DayIqbal Day

Guest Speaker Sessions

Inspirational talks by professionals, alumni, scientists, and leaders — expanding students' horizons and career awareness.

WebinarsAlumni TalksCareer Guidance
Why It Matters

Benefits of Co-Curricular Activities

Holistic Development

Activities complement academics to produce well-rounded, balanced individuals ready for life beyond school.

Teamwork & Leadership

Inter-house competitions and clubs teach students how to lead, collaborate and resolve conflict.

Confidence Building

Stage performances, debates and presentations build students' self-confidence and public speaking ability.

Social Responsibility

Community service programs develop empathy, civic sense and a spirit of giving back to society.
