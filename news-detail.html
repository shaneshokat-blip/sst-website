SST Proudly Hosted the 78th Pakistan Independence Day 2025 Grand Celebration

August 14, 2025 SST Communications Team Rashidabad Campus Events

In a magnificent display of patriotism, unity, and pride, SST Public School Rashidabad hosted a grand celebration marking the 78th Independence Day of Pakistan on August 14, 2025. The event brought together students, faculty, parents, and members of the Rashidabad community in a unique celebration that united multiple institutions under one flag.

A Unified Celebration

This year's Independence Day celebration was exceptional — SST jointly hosted the event with sister institutions including Rashidabad Community School, IDA RIUE (Rashidabad Urban Institute of Education), and Deaf Reach School. This inter-institutional collaboration embodied the spirit of unity and inclusivity that defines the Rashidabad community.

"Today we celebrate not just the birth of a nation, but the promise of its future — and that future stands here before us, in every student at SST." — The Principal, SST Public School Rashidabad

Programme Highlights

The celebration was filled with colour, energy, and deep national sentiment. The programme included:

• Grand flag hoisting ceremony at sunrise with the national anthem
• Patriotic speeches by students from all participating institutions
• Tableaux showcasing Pakistan's history and heroes
• Nasheed and national song competitions
• A special tribute to the armed forces and martyrs of Pakistan
• Community meal shared by all participants as a symbol of brotherhood

Students Shine Bright

Yarians (SST students) demonstrated outstanding preparation and pride during the event. Their speeches, performances, and discipline were widely praised by attendees and community leaders who were present at the celebration.

The event concluded with a heartfelt dua for Pakistan's prosperity, unity, peace, and continued progress. SST Public School Rashidabad remains committed to producing graduates who carry the flag of Pakistan with honour wherever they go.
