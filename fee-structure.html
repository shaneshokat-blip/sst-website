Important Notes:

• Fees must be paid before the due date to avoid late payment fines (Rs. 100 per day).

• The Security Deposit of Rs. 35,000 is fully refundable at the time of leaving the school.

• Cambridge International Examination (CAIE) fees are charged separately as per official CAIE rates.

• Re-admission fee of Rs. 60,000 is applicable if a student leaves and wishes to re-join.

• All fees are subject to revision. Contact the school office for confirmation before payment.
