Residential Excellence

A Home Away From Home

The residential life at SST Public School Rashidabad is the cornerstone of our educational philosophy. The boarding houses are more than just dormitories; they are vibrant communities where students learn the values of brotherhood, discipline, and leadership.

The school features a total of 11 purpose-built boarding houses, each designed to provide a comfortable and academically conducive environment. Every house is a miniature society where seniors mentor juniors, fostering a unique culture of mutual respect and Sargodhian spirit.

Supervision & Management

Each boarding house is meticulously supervised by a dedicated pastoral care team:

  • House Master (HM)
  • Assistant House Master (AHM)
  • Experienced Matron
  • Dedicated House Bearers
