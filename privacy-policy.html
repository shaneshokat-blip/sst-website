Privacy Policy

How SST Public School Rashidabad handles your information.

HomePrivacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2026  |  This policy applies to sstpsr.edu.pk and all SST digital platforms.

1. Introduction

SST Public School Rashidabad ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, and visitors who interact with our website and digital platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you fill our inquiry or contact form.
  • Admission Information: Details submitted through the admission application form.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type (via analytics tools).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and admission applications
  • To send relevant school updates and newsletters (opt-in only)
  • To improve the website and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared with:

  • School administration for admission processing
  • Formspree (contact form provider) — subject to their privacy policy
  • Government authorities if required by law

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve user experience (e.g., remembering preferences). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We encourage you to contact us directly for sensitive matters.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at info@sstpsr.edu.pk for any such requests.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., Cambridge CAIE, Formspree). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
