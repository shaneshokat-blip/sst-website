IT Department & Smart Classrooms

The Information Technology Department at SST Public School Rashidabad is equipped with modern computers, high-speed internet, and smart boards that transform every classroom into an interactive digital environment.

The Learning Management System (LMS) enables teachers to assign tasks, share resources, and conduct assessments digitally — keeping students, teachers and parents connected seamlessly.

Our IT department offers structured courses in Computer Science, Information Technology, and coding — from beginner to advanced levels aligned with Cambridge IGCSE curriculum.

IT Department Features

Smart Classrooms

Interactive smart boards and projectors in every classroom for dynamic lessons.

High-Speed Internet

Campus-wide high-speed internet enabling e-learning and digital research.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with 40+ workstations for IT classes and self-study.

LMS Platform

Digital Learning Management System connecting students, teachers and parents.

Coding Classes

Structured programming classes from Scratch to Python for all age groups.

Digital Safety

Cybersecurity awareness and responsible internet usage taught to all students.
