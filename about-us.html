Who We Are

A Dream Realized for Pakistan's Future

Rashidabad is the realization of the dream of two welfare organizations in Pakistan, though radically different from each other but working hand in hand for one common goal: prosperity of the 'have-nots' of the country.

SST Public School Rashidabad (Sargodhian Spirit Trust) was established with the noble mission of providing world-class education to deserving students from all backgrounds. Over 19+ years, we have become one of Pakistan's most respected residential schools.

Our residential campus provides a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills — preparing students to become responsible citizens of Pakistan and contributors to the global community.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading center of academic excellence in Pakistan, producing graduates who are intellectually capable, morally upright, spiritually grounded, and equipped to serve their nation and humanity.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in a disciplined, inclusive environment that nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and character — enabling students to excel academically and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Journey

A Legacy of 19+ Years

2004 – Founding

School Established

SST Public School Rashidabad was founded by Sargodhian Spirit Trust to provide quality education to underprivileged students.

2008

Academic Excellence

First batch of students achieved outstanding Cambridge results, putting SST on the map of Pakistan's top schools.

2014

Infrastructure Expansion

New Science Laboratories, Library & Documentation Centre, and modern hostels were added to the campus.

2019

Digital Transformation

Introduction of the Learning Management System (LMS) and digitally-equipped classrooms for 21st century learning.

2023

AI Lab Launch

Launch of the AI-integrated Learning Lab — one of the first in Pakistani schools — empowering students with future-ready skills.

2025+

Continued Excellence

Continuing to grow, innovate, and inspire with record admissions and outstanding academic results.
