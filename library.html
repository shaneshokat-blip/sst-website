Library & Documentation Centre

The Library and Documentation Centre at SST Public School Rashidabad is a rich repository of knowledge — housing thousands of books, journals, encyclopedias, and digital resources.

Students are encouraged to visit regularly for independent study, reading, and research. The library also subscribes to international digital databases providing access to academic journals and e-books.

The reading culture at SST is deeply embedded — weekly library sessions are part of the academic schedule for all classes.
