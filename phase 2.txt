# 2.5 STEP 2 — KEEP / MERGE / ARCHIVE / REBUILD DECISION

Ab har page par decision lo.

Har original page ke samne 1 فیصلہ hona chahiye:

* **KEEP** → same concept ke saath new site mein ayega
* **MERGE** → kisi aur page ke andar combine hoga
* **ARCHIVE** → public menu se niklega, but record mein rahega
* **REBUILD** → new structure/design ke saath re-create hoga
* **EXTERNAL** → external link hi rahega ya later replace hoga

## Example thinking:

* `Principal Message` → KEEP ya About ke andar MERGE
* `Download Prospectus` → Downloads center mein MERGE
* `Fee Structure` → Admissions ke andar KEEP as dedicated section/page
* `Facilities individual pages` → REBUILD as reusable template pages
* `Old news posts` → Content Entries mein migrate
* `Broken/empty page` → ARCHIVE or client approval

# 2.12 STEP 9 — URL STRATEGY

New URLs clean aur future-proof honi chahiye.

## Rules

* lowercase
* kebab-case
* short but clear
* no random dates unless news
* avoid WordPress-style messy slugs

## Example Structure

* `/about`
* `/about/principals-message`
* `/admissions`
* `/admissions/how-to-apply`
* `/admissions/fee-structure`
* `/academics`
* `/campus-life/facilities/science-labs`
* `/news`
* `/news/[slug]`
* `/downloads`
* `/contact`

## Redirect Planning

Old URL → new URL mapping list banegi, taake later migration ya SEO safe rahe.
