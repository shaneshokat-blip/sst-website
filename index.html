About Our School

Inspiring Futures Through Holistic Education

Rashidabad is the realization of the dream of two welfare organizations in Pakistan — radically different from each other but working hand in hand for one common goal: prosperity of the 'have-nots' of the country.

Our Mission: Commitment to educating young people to become responsible citizens and leaders of Pakistan and the world, based on the premise that every human being is a person of worth. We nurture the Sargodhian Spirit — discipline, excellence, and service.

Our programs are designed to:

Encourage critical thinking and creativity.

Promote a love for lifelong learning.

Build strong character and leadership skills.

Our Faculties

Fostering Brilliance, Nurturing Dreams

Our distinguished faculty departments offer world-class education across diverse disciplines.

Department of Sciences

Cultivating curiosity and innovation through hands-on exploration in physics, chemistry, and biology to shape the scientists and innovators of tomorrow.

Department of Humanities

Helping students understand their culture, values, and society through Pakistan Studies, Islamiyat, and Urdu, preparing them to be responsible individuals.

Department of Information Technology

Empowering students with cutting-edge AI knowledge and skills to innovate and excel in the technology-driven world.

Department of Mathematical Sciences

Fostering analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities through the exploration of pure and applied mathematics.

Department of English

Inspiring excellence in communication, critical thinking, and creative expression for global academic and professional success.

Department of Commerce

Equipping future business leaders with expertise in finance, economics, and entrepreneurship to thrive in competitive markets.
