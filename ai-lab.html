AI-Integrated Learning Lab

Recently, SST Public School Rashidabad introduced an AI-integrated Learning Lab — equipped with advanced software designed to personalize learning for each student.

This initiative positions SST as one of the pioneering schools in Pakistan to adopt artificial intelligence in education, giving our students a significant advantage in the technology-driven world.

Students learn machine learning concepts, AI-powered tools, robotics programming, and future skills that will define careers in the 21st century.
