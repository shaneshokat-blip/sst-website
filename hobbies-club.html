Hobbies Club

Every student has a unique talent. At SST, we nurture it through structured hobby programs.

HomeCampus LifeHobbies Club
12 Active Clubs

Find Your Passion

Students join hobby clubs based on their interests. Each club meets weekly with a dedicated teacher-mentor to guide growth.

Photography

Capture moments, learn composition and storytelling through the lens.

Gardening

Grow vegetables and flowers in our school garden — learn sustainability.

Coding Club

Build websites, apps, and games. Explore the world of programming.

Reading Circle

Weekly book discussions, literature reviews, and creative writing.

Painting & Art

Express creativity through colours, canvases and mixed media art.

Chess Club

Strategic thinking and problem-solving through the royal game of chess.

Astronomy

Stargazing sessions, telescope practice and exploration of space science.

Creative Writing

Urdu/English poetry, short stories, essays and literary expression.

First Aid & Health

Basic first aid, CPR training, and health awareness workshops.

Film & Media

Script writing, video editing, and student documentary projects.

Robotics

Build and program robots — hands-on engineering and AI exploration.

Environmental Club

Tree plantation drives, recycling campaigns, and eco-awareness projects.

All SST students are encouraged to join at least one hobby club. Joining is simple:

3

Attend the first session and meet your club mentor
