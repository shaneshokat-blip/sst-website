Application Process

Step-by-Step Guide

Check Eligibility

Ensure your child meets the age and academic requirements for the class they are applying to. Review our eligibility criteria carefully before proceeding.

First Step

Download/Collect Application Form

Download the admission form from our website or collect it from the school office during working hours (8 AM – 4 PM, Mon–Sat).

Second Step

Fill the Application Form

Complete all sections of the application form accurately. Attach required documents including academic records, photographs, and B-Form copy.

Third Step

Submit the Application

Submit your completed application form along with all required documents to the admission office. Online submissions are also accepted via our official website.

Fourth Step

Appear in Entry Test

All candidates must appear in the written entry test. Test dates will be communicated after form submission. Prepare using our Foundation Year and Pre-Camb Sample Papers.

Fifth Step

Result & Enrollment

Successful candidates will be notified via SMS/email. Upon selection, complete the enrollment process and submit the required fee within the stipulated time.

Final Step
Documents Required

Required Documents

Child's B-Form (NADRA)
Previous Academic Reports / Marksheets
Passport-size Photographs (4)
Parent/Guardian CNIC copies
Completed Application Form
Application Processing Fee (receipt)
