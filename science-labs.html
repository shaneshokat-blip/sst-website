State-of-the-Art Science Labs

The School houses purpose-built, fully equipped, and state-of-the-art science laboratories, established as a part of Sciences curriculum to provide hands-on learning experience.

Our three major laboratories — Physics, Chemistry, and Biology — are equipped with modern instruments, safety equipment, and digital tools that bring theoretical concepts to life.

Students regularly conduct experiments, participate in science exhibitions, and represent SST in national science competitions — winning top positions consistently.

Lab Highlights

Physics Lab

Equipped with advanced instruments for electricity, optics, mechanics and thermodynamics experiments.

Chemistry Lab

Fully equipped with fume hoods, digital balances, glassware, and chemicals for O-Level coursework.

Biology Lab

Microscopes, slide kits, anatomy models and dissection equipment for comprehensive biological study.

Safety Standards

First aid kits, safety goggles, fire extinguishers and proper ventilation throughout all labs.

Expert Supervision

All experiments supervised by qualified, experienced science teachers and lab assistants.

Competition Ready

Students prepare for national and international science competitions in our advanced lab environment.
